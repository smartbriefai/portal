What it does

Everything before
the doctor walks in

Four capabilities. One workflow. Zero extra admin for your staff.

AI clinical brief
Patient fills a structured intake form before arrival. AI converts it into a specialty-specific clinical brief - history, complaints, and context structured for how you think.
Pre-consultation
Drug safety flags
Automatic interaction checks across 82 India-market drugs. Contraindications and allergy alerts surface before you prescribe - not after the patient has left.
Patient safety
AI Rx intelligence
Prescription suggestions based on complaint, patient history, and drug safety - reviewed and signed by you. Cuts prescription time without cutting clinical judgment.
AI-assisted
Voice notes
Dictate clinical notes during or after consultation. Transcribed, timestamped, and attached to the patient record automatically.
Hands-free
Patient queue
See your full day at a glance - who has completed intake, who is pending, which patients have flagged conditions requiring attention before they enter.
OPD workflow
Follow-up workflow
Automated follow-up reminders sent to patients after consultation. Review responses before the next visit - no manual tracking required.

The workflow

Four steps. Zero friction.

1
Patient receives link
Sent via WhatsApp or SMS before appointment. Opens on any phone - no app needed.
