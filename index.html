How It Works

From arrival to brief in 60 seconds.

Front desk registers the patient. AI generates the brief. Doctor receives context before the patient walks in.

1

Patient Arrives

Front desk registers name and chief complaint — or uploads an old prescription/report. Takes under 60 seconds.

2

AI Generates Brief

SmartBrief structures the input into a clean, scannable brief. Flags allergies. Extracts key history. Suggests investigations.

3

Doctor Receives

The brief appears on the doctor's screen before the patient enters. No fumbling through papers. No starting from zero.

4

Smarter Consult

Doctor starts with context. Allergy flags are visible. Suggested actions are pre-loaded. Consultation is faster and more informed.
